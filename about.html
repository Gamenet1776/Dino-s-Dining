Usual Experience

When you take your first step in our diner, you may be excited or frightened by those huge dinosaurs scattered throughout the place. No need to worry, they aren’t animate, and if they happen to be, life finds a way. Typically, there isn’t a long line because only reservations are allowed. Except, if you arrive more than 15 minutes late, you have failed your very first task and as a punishment, you’ll have to make a new reservation. Once you are introduced to your seat(s), you’ll only have to wait a minute until your order arrives fresh and delicious. You may fear showing bad table manners. No need to worry, there’s no wrong way to eat! If you want to eat like a real dinosaur, go ahead, we’ll happily cheer you on. If you rather not, that’s completely fine, we still appreciate you for choosing us as your hosts.
